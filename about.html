LOORRNZO

Lorenzo Lo (Lorrnnzo)



Lorenzo is an aspiring and professional photographer/filmmaker based in Sydney, Australia. Update: Because of international travel restrictions, he currently works as a freelancer in Guangzhou, China.

As a kid growing up in the city with beautiful skyline and skyscrapers, I always wanted to find a way to explore Guangzhou and that's the reason why I immediately fell in love with the process of shooting urban landscape and neon/film portraits, when I first picked up my camera and started taking photos and videos back in 2017. After years of shooting, color grading and practicing, I finally found my own shooting & editing style and created my very first preset pack! They are all available to you now!

Though based in Sydney/Guangzhou, I'm available for editorial, commercial, portrait, documentary, and travel photography/video assignments worldwide.

"There is a lot of good content on the internet right now, and I'd be happy if I'm one of the few photographers who can inspire you :)"
